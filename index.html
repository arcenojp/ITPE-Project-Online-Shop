Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can browse our menu, select the items you want, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your delivery address and payment information to complete your order.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver daily from 11AM to 10PM. Orders placed outside these hours will be delivered the next business day.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 15 minutes of placing it by calling our customer service. After that, the order enters preparation and cannot be changed.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We offer catering for events of all sizes. Please contact us at least 72 hours in advance to discuss your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Cash on delivery is also available in select areas.
